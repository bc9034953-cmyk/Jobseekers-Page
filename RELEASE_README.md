## Play Store store listing descriptions 

Welcome to Job Seekers Page!

Job Seekers Page is designed by experienced professionals to make the job search and hiring process easier, faster, and more efficient. We offer a comprehensive platform for both job seekers and employers, providing advanced search features, application tracking, and robust tools for managing job postings and candidates.

Features for Job Seekers:
- Vast database of job opportunities across industries and locations
- Advanced search filters (job type, industry, location, salary, etc.)
- Application tracking and notifications
- Create candidate profiles and upload resumes

Features for Employers:
- Quick and easy job postings
- Applicant Tracking System (ATS) for managing applications
- Resume search to find qualified candidates
- Create and manage company profiles
- View candidate listings and resumes
- Purchase plans for enhanced access to candidate profiles

Additional Features:
- Dual login options for candidates and employers
- Seamless switching between user types
- Commitment to transparency, inclusivity, and equal opportunities
